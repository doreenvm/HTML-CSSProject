Chocolate Muffins Recipe

Super moist chocolate muffins made with cocoa powder and LOTS of chocolate chips! Enjoy these rich and decadent muffins for breakfast or dessert!

This delicious chocolate muffin recipe is a family favorite.

When it comes to grab and go breakfasts or pick-me-ups, these muffins always do the trick.

Ingredients

  • 250g self-raising flour
  • 25g cocoa powder
  • 2tsp baking powder
  • 175g caster sugar(or 125g caster sugar ang 50g muscovado)
  • 175g milk
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 100ml mild olive oil(or you can use sunflower oil if too strong-flovoured)
  • 75g plain chocolate chunks
  • 1-2tsp vanilla extract
